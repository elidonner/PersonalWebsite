Dove

Role: PCB design and fabrication, mechanical design and prototyping, embedded software engineer, human-factors interviewer, project-presentations/fundraising.

Type: Start-up

Time: January 2021 - September 2022

Dove is a startup I worked on during my time at the University of Pennsylvania. It has spun off as a full company, Altrumed. Altrumed is creating the first wearable autoinjector capable of sensing an opioid overdose and automatically injecting naloxone. The device senses an opioid overdose by measuring blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) and accelerometer values. When an overdose is sensed, it alerts the user before injecting life-saving naloxone.
